We Deliver!
We work closely with all of our farm partners, and take pride in bringing you their local, all natural products.

Kinderhook Farm
Kinderhook Farm is one of the largest producers of 100% grass-fed beef and lamb in the Hudson Valley. With over 1,000 acres of rolling hay meadows and pastures, they raise their cattle and sheep on a 100% grass and legume diet that includes no grain, antibiotics, growth hormones or animal by-products. To learn more about Kinderhook, click here.

Meili Farm
Meili Farm's Tamworth-Berkshire hogs are all raised on pasture in the most humane way possible. All of their hogs are bred, born and raised on the farm, and are hand-fed twice daily a mix of natural grains, vegetables, hay and goat milk whey from a local cheesemaker. To learn more about Meili, click here.

Autumn's Harvest Farm
At Autumn's Harvest Farm, all of the animals are rotated daily to fresh grazing areas. In addition to this they are fed high quality natural feed ensuring the healthiest possible outcome without jeopardizing the animals quality of life. Autumn's Harvest prides themselves on being a family friendly farm that is dedicated to their animals and to their consumers. To learn more about Autumn's Harvest, click here.

Arcadian Pastures
Arcadian Pastures is a small family-run heritage breed farm in Schoharie county, NY. Their belief is that animals raised outdoors, on high quality pasture, and that are treated with respect, produce the most nutritionally-dense and best tasting meats. All of their meats are hormone and antibiotic free and are never exposed to herbicides or pesticides. To learn more about Arcadian Pastures, click here
